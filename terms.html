TERMS

There are several statuses that an order may currently be. These have specific names that are referenced throughout the rest of this agreement. Here is a list of the relevant order status names and what they mean:

  • Automatically Cancelled: Services ordered on our platform are automatically cancelled if not enough providers claim the order. The time at which this happens is the midpoint between when the order was placed and when it was scheduled to take place.
  • Customer Cancelled: Customers have the right to cancel any order up until the time that the order is started by the provider. See our payment section for more information about this.
  • Provider Cancelled: Providers can cancel any order so long as they have not yet started the order.
  • Refunded: Providers can choose to refund the customer in the event that they personally feel their work as inadequate. This can be done on our platform after they have stopped the order.
  • Pending: An order that has just been placed and is waiting to be claimed.
  • Claimed: After an order is placed by the customer, providers can respond to email/text notifications to indicate they would like to complete the service. After the number of providers who have responded is equal to the number of providers requested by the customer, the service is considered claimed.
  • Started: Providers start orders to indicate that they have begun completing the service.
  • Ended: Providers end orders to indicate that they have stopped working on the service. Providers can also pause services to indicate that they have temporarily stopped providing the service. "Working time" as mentioned later is defined as the time between the start time and end time, not including any paused time.
  • Marked Completed: Providers mark orders as completed on our platform when they feel that they have adequately provided the service. Our platform automatically marks orders as completed 24 hours after they attain a status of "ended". Payment remains on hold in the HelpHog Stripe account until either: the order maintains this status for 24 hours, or the customer indicates they are happy with the service by marking the order as completed on our platform.
  • Disputed: After a service is marked completed by the provider, customers will have the opportunity to dispute an order if they feel that the service was inadequate or the final price is incorrect. Providers have the opportunity to resolve the order in whatever way they see fit without the involvement of HelpHog, and then mark the order as completed again. If the order is disputed several times by the customer, it will remain in this status and our informal negotiation process will take effect, described in more detail elsewhere.
  • Paid: The funds that were initially placed on hold within the customer's bank account have been transferred from the HelpHog Stripe account to the provider. Order is resolved at this point, and is excluded from our informal negotiation. Order may not enter any of the order statuses from here.
